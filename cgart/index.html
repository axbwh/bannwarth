CG Art

Drawing inspiration from both natural and architectural formations, patterns and motifs, a mixture of digital sculpture and procedural modelling was employed in the creation of these intricate illustrations. Each artwork looks to reconstruct, remix and re-contextualise such structures into surreal yet seemingly photoreal cg renders.
